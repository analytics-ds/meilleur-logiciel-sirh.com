> **In short:**
> 1. Digitizing reviews saves managers 30 to 50% of time on average.
> 2. Dedicated software ensures compliance with mandatory career reviews every two years.
> 3. Collected data feeds workforce planning and training plans.
> 4. Specialized talent solutions often outperform reviews modules of generalist HRIS.

## Why digitize annual reviews

Annual and career reviews are still often managed through Word templates exchanged by email, with no centralization or tracking. This approach complicates HR oversight, weakens legal compliance and wastes managers' and HR teams' time. Dedicated software changes the game entirely.

## Features to expect

Solid review software offers customizable templates, e-signature, commitment tracking and alignment with goals and skills. Advanced solutions integrate continuous feedback, OKR management and development plan tracking.

- Templates configurable by employee group
- Validation workflow manager / HR / employee
- E-signature of review records
- Commitment and goal tracking
- HR dashboards by team and site

## Benefits for workforce planning

Data from reviews directly feeds workforce planning. Workforce views by role, identification of training needs, detection of high potentials: the software turns an administrative obligation into a strategic management lever.

## Generalist HRIS or specialized solution

Generalist HRIS often include a basic reviews module. Dedicated talent platforms offer a more mature experience: 360 feedback, LMS integration, internal mobility alignment. The choice depends on HR maturity and budget.
